Welcome to Tshedimosetso Youth Network

About Us

Welcome to Tshedimosetso Youth Network. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of children and youth. Our mission is to empower the next generation by providing educational resources, nutritious meals, and a safe space for mentorship and personal growth.

Our Mission

Our goal is to provide the support and opportunities needed for children to thrive in all aspects of their lives. Through dedicated volunteers and partners, we strive to create an environment that fosters learning, well-being, and a sense of community.

Our Approach

We focus on three core areas: education, nutrition & well-being, and mentorship. We believe that every child deserves the opportunity to succeed and grow in a supportive environment that nurtures their potential.

Why We Exist

Many children in underserved communities face numerous challenges, including limited access to education and resources. By working together with local schools, community groups, and volunteers, we provide the support these children need to achieve a brighter future.

Our Services

Children Education

We provide afterschool tutoring, homework assistance, and access to educational resources to ensure no child is left behind. Our volunteers and educators work closely with students to boost their confidence and academic performance..

Nutrition & Well-being

Healthy children learn better. We provide nutritious meals and snacks to ensure children stay focused and energized. Many kids rely on our center as a safe space where they can receive proper meals and health checkups.

Safe Spaces & Mentorship

Our drop-in center provides a welcoming space where children can feel secure, receive guidance, and find mentors who inspire them.
